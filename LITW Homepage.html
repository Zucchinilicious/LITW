Return To Possum Springs!

Love in the Woods is the upcoming Night in the Woods Dating Simulator.
Taking place 4 years after the events of Night in the woods, Love in the Woods is setting out to get you more intimately aquainted with the citizens of Possum Springs. You're the new kid on block in Possum Springs, and it's time to get out and mingle. You'll soon find yourself in several relationships with the characters from Night in the woods.
